**IconButton** — square, icon-only button for toolbars, close affordances, and inline controls. Always supply `label` for accessibility.

```jsx
<IconButton label="نسخ" variant="ghost">⧉</IconButton>
<IconButton label="حذف" variant="secondary">🗑</IconButton>
```

Variants: `ghost`, `solid` (navy), `accent` (turquoise), `secondary` (outlined). Sizes `sm | md | lg`.
